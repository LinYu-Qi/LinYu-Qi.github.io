🎰 FlipPay Campaign: User Activation & ROI Optimization

Company: IGO | Duration: Feb 2024 | Campaign Type: User Activation Driver

The Challenge

The platform launched a FlipPay task campaign to drive user activation and engagement. The challenge was to validate campaign effectiveness, measure ROI across different task types (login vs. purchase), and understand the structural impact on user participation and purchase behavior.

My Approach

I conducted a comprehensive campaign effectiveness analysis, comparing user participation across login and purchase tasks, analyzing purchase penetration rates, and calculating ROI by task type. I examined the relationship between reward intensity and participant numbers, and evaluated the short-term and long-term revenue impact of the campaign.

Results

Betting Penetration Rate
36% on campaign day (vs. 25% baseline)
7-Day ROI
60.5% based on net betting increase of 5,873M
Campaign Positioning
User Expansion Activation-driven, not high-value conversion
Key Insight
Positive Correlation Between reward strength and participation

Key Findings

  • Campaign successfully positioned as user activation driver, not high-value conversion mechanism
  • Clear positive correlation between reward intensity and participant volume
  • Purchase task ROI (60.5%) driven primarily by high participation rates
  • Campaign effectively improved touch rate and user engagement across platform
  • Short-term revenue gains validated campaign effectiveness despite acquisition-focused positioning
